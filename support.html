Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I use the app?

Simply open the app, input a keyword that you want to find,
and point your camera at the target area, move your phone slowly that makes camera focus better,
at the same time you can zoom in and out using the slider, the app will help you locate and identify it.

2. Does the app collect any personal data?

No, our app does not collect, store, or share any user data.
